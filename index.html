Overview

I am a Research Scientist at OpenAI working at the intersection of machine learning and robotics. My research focuses on applying deep reinforcement learning, generative models, and synthetic data to problems in robotic perception and control.

I am working on my PhD in Computer Science at UC Berkeley advised by Pieter Abbeel.

Additionally, I co-organize a machine learning training program for engineers who want to learn about production-ready deep learning called Full Stack Deep Learning.

In the past, I have been a management consultant at McKinsey and an Investment Partner at Dorm Room Fund.
